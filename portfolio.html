Inventory Management Platform

The idea of the project came when I was organizing the store room in my house and realised how cumbersome it was to keep track of the items. The frontend is written with Typescript and React, backend is built with Express which is also in Typescript. Database is Postgres and Sequelize is used as the ORM, Docker is also used for development. Developing in Typescript takes much longer than in Javascript but I feel that the benefits far outweighs the costs, especially when you are developing a production level project. It helps you spot silly or potential bugs beforehand and also gives you a nice autocomplete.
